  9. Advocacy for accessibility
    1. Tips on promoting accessibility
      1. TIP 1: Check whether the country has ratified the Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities
      2. TIP 2: Explain how promoting and adhering to accessibility will help realize the Sustainable Development Goals and the commitments to “leave no one behind”
      3. TIP 3: Check the accessibility requirements in national building codes, regulations and disability- related policy/legislation
      4. TIP 4: Partner with organizations of persons with disabilities as key allies
      5. TIP 5: Engage donors and development partners to develop or implement organizational policies on disability and accessibility
      6. TIP 6: Share examples of affordable and sustainable accessible facilities
    2. Frequently asked questions on accessibility (FAQ)
    3. Misconceptions vs facts
      1. M1: It is too expensive.
      2. M2: Very few people with disabilities need access.
      3. M3: Accessibility only benefits people with disabilities.
      4. M4: Countries do not need to integrate accessibility standards.
      5. M5: Engaging with people with disabilities requires expert knowledge.
